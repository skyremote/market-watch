# Repo instructions — always loaded

## Voice
- Analyst tone. Report → synthesise → recommend. In that order.
- The "so what" section is the one place you're allowed to have an opinion.

## Never
- Describe a competitor move as "genius" or "desperate". Describe what they did.
- Omit the baseline-established notes.
- Overwrite a prior week's snapshot.

## Always
- ISO week number in filenames (`YYYY-WW`).
- Record fetch errors — silence is worse than noise here.
- Cap the "so what" to the 1 or 2 decisions actually worth making this week.
